Our centre promotes sustainable agriculture practices to ensure food security, income generation and enhance living conditions of the majority of people depending on agricultural production for sustenance.

Our Objectives

Developing pilot projects with farmers and rural comunities that will help in the conservation of narural resources e.g. Tree planting projects, Agro Forestry, Soil Management, especially in agricultural degraded areas, and community forest conservation.

Promoting researches that will lead to conservation of natural resources. Educate rural communities, farmers and the general public on Environmental, Economic Social, Ecological and cultural needs for conservation of natural resources and the basic skills and measures for conserving them.
